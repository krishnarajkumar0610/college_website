Dr. P. Umarani, Co-Ordinator

Mr. K. Dhakshnamoorthy, Co-Ordinator

The Career Guidance Cell was started in 2003. The cell prepares students for campus interviews and job placement and trains students for competitive and civil service exams. It arranges training programs on soft skill development and provides career counseling. The cell organizes workshops and seminars on personality development, interpersonal relationships, communication skills, and presentation skills to enable all-round development of individuals. It provides information on professional development courses and higher studies available in India and abroad. The cell also conducts training programs on behavioral skills and stress management, ultimately helping students discover their strengths, sharpen their interests, and plan their future.
