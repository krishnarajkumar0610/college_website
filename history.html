College since its inception

Thiagarajar College founded on 7th July 1949, was formally inaugurated on 12th October 1949.

The college offers 28 UG, 12 PG, 11 M.Phil, and 10 Ph.D. programs, along with diploma and certificate courses.

It is a private, aided co-educational institution affiliated with Madurai Kamaraj University.

Accredited with ‘A’ Grade by NAAC multiple times, the college focuses on employability, research, and student development.

The Alumni Charitable Trust plays a crucial role in the college’s development.
