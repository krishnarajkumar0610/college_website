IQAC, Thiagarajar College was established as per the guidelines of NAAC to coordinate the functioning of the institution. It acts as a nodal agency to accelerate the administrative and academic progression of the institute. It facilitates performance evaluation, assessment, accreditation and quality enhancement process. It serves to sustain and enhance post-accreditation quality.
